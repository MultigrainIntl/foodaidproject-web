How it works

From your selections to a formal quote

The Mixed Truckload Builder is a free online tool that lets you design your own bulk dry-goods order. It is a request tool — submitting it starts a conversation, not a binding order.

1

Pick your products

Choose from oatmeal, beans, lentils, peas, rice, and cereal. The tool builds your truckload in real time as you add items.

2

Watch the load fill

See pallets used, total net weight, and room remaining — so your truck is filled efficiently before you submit.

3

Request your quote

Submit your organization and delivery details. Food Aid Project reviews the request and follows up when current product, pricing, freight and organizational capacity can be confirmed.
