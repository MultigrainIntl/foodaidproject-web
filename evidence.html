Claim classifications

Plain language protects trust.

Proven

Supported by an identified primary document, independently published source or directly observable tool.

Historical

Accurate for a stated period but not presented as current activity.

In development

A real initiative being built, tested or organized. It is not described as fully operational or scaled.

Paused or canceled

Oatmeal 4 Hope is currently in this category.

Unknown

Not published as fact until missing evidence or status is resolved.

Our rule

A goal is never presented as an achieved result.
