Governance

Governance is being strengthened.

Food Aid Project is rebuilding its board and formalizing the next stage of its work.

Existing bylaws and policies address conflicts of interest, compensation, ethics, gifts, whistleblowing and nondiscrimination. We will update this page as board appointments and current records are completed.

Organizational responsibility

The nonprofit is responsible for its own work.

Food Aid Project, Inc. is responsible for its charitable mission, programs, fundraising and partnerships.

Any outside service, technology access or financial arrangement must be documented and reviewed through appropriate nonprofit safeguards.

Use of support

Support helps us build useful work responsibly.

Donations may support nonprofit operations, program development, partnerships, research and approved mission-related tools.

If a gift is restricted to a particular purpose, we will describe that purpose clearly. We will not promise a specific result unless the program and funding are structured to support it.
