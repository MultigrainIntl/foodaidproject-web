What partners can expect

Start with the real question.

We will be clear about current capacity, what is still being built and where another organization may be better positioned to help.

The strongest collaborations have a defined problem, useful knowledge on both sides and a practical next step.

A good first message includes

The challenge: What needs to change?

The setting: Who is affected and where?

The decision: What choice or missing information is holding the work back?

Your role: What knowledge, access or capability can you bring?
