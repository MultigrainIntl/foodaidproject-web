Our approach

Start with people and the problem they are trying to solve.

The best partnerships begin with listening. We want to understand the people involved, the choices they face and what useful help would look like.

We will be honest about what Food Aid Project can do now, what we are still building and where another organization may be better suited. When technology is useful, it should strengthen the people doing the work—not replace their knowledge, judgment or relationships.

Start a conversation

How would you like to help—or what challenge can we help you address?

You do not need a finished proposal. Tell us who you serve, what you are trying to change and what kind of help or collaboration you have in mind.
