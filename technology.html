Technology & intelligence

Useful intelligence should not depend on the size of your technology budget.

Food Aid Project’s technology direction is about access: bringing weather, crop, geospatial, freight and market context into decisions that strengthen food and economic security.

The simple version

See more. Decide earlier. Use resources better.

The original software idea combined product price with freight to show delivered cost. The broader direction applies the same principle across the food system: decisions improve when people can see the relevant conditions together.

Weather and early warning

Connect changing conditions and hazards with agricultural production, food availability, logistics and preparedness.

Crop and field intelligence

Explore crop geography, development, soil and field context, yield indicators and the ground truth required to make models credible.

Procurement and market intelligence

Combine product, supplier, timing and freight knowledge so organizations can evaluate delivered cost and opportunity more effectively.
