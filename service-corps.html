Skills for Food Security Service Corps

The hours are yours.

Tell us what you know, what you do well, what you care about, and what you already love to do. We use that information to explore individualized ways you may be able to advance food and economic security.

Our foundational principle

Community service should not erase the individual. Whenever practical, we aim to match real Food Aid Project needs with a participant's useful skills, interests, experience, relationships, and authentic enthusiasm.

Interest and pre-qualification

This questionnaire is for people interested in volunteering, applying professional skills, completing eligible community-service hours, becoming a mission ambassador or strategic connector, mentoring participants, referring candidates, or exploring an organizational partnership.

Food Aid Project may use your responses to identify possible opportunities now or in the future and to follow up when an appropriate project, jurisdictional pathway, or partnership becomes available.

Important program status: submitting this questionnaire does not create a placement, approve community-service credit, establish an attorney-client relationship, or guarantee acceptance by a court, probation office, or other authority. Do not begin counting hours unless Food Aid Project and the applicable supervising authority have approved a written service plan.
Please do not submit sensitive legal or personal information. Do not include charges, alleged conduct, case numbers, Social Security numbers, dates of birth, medical information, financial account information, passwords, or confidential legal communications. Initial qualification does not require those details.

What happens next

  • You tell us what you can contribute and what would make service meaningful.
  • Your responses are prepared as a structured email for you to review and send.
  • Food Aid Project may contact you about a suitable opportunity or next-stage screening.
  • No court-related hours begin until all required written approvals are in place.
