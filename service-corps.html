Tell us how you would like to contribute

This questionnaire is a starting point for remote volunteers, skilled professionals, mentors, connectors and people exploring eligible community-service work or an organizational partnership.

Food Aid Project may use your responses to identify possible opportunities now or in the future and to follow up when an appropriate remote project, jurisdictional pathway, outreach initiative, or partnership becomes available.

Remote-only operating model: Food Aid Project does not currently manage in-person volunteer programs, food processing, packing, warehouse shifts, distribution-line work, or event staffing. Current opportunities are remote and knowledge-, relationship-, communication-, research-, or technology-based.
Important program status: submitting this questionnaire does not create a placement, approve community-service credit, establish an attorney-client relationship, or guarantee acceptance by a court, probation office, or other authority. Do not begin counting hours unless Food Aid Project and the applicable supervising authority have approved a written service plan.
Please do not submit sensitive legal or personal information. Do not include charges, alleged conduct, case numbers, Social Security numbers, dates of birth, medical information, financial account information, passwords, or confidential legal communications.

What happens next

  • You tell us what you can contribute and what would make service meaningful.
  • We classify the inquiry and identify possible remote matches.
  • When the secure intake backend is active, you receive a reference number and acknowledgment automatically.
  • Food Aid Project may contact you about a suitable opportunity or next-stage screening.
  • No court-related hours begin until all required written approvals are in place.
